Our Model

Client approaches NRHD Resources with a project plan.

A senior consultant examines the project plan and compares the human resources required to complete the task against NRHD's talent database.

A quotation is presented to the client.

Once project commences, NRHD will allocate a project manager who will serve as the single point of contact between client and NRHD resources.
